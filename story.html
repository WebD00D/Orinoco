Our Story

Born from a passion.

Wade cherishes the memories of sitting on the front porch listening to baseball games on the radio with his grandfather, and early on learned to appreciate the art of a fine smoke from his grandfather. Subsequently, Wade served as a naval flight officer, campus minister, underwater photographer, business executive, and for the last 36 years as a professor of law.

A cigar enthusiast from a young age, Kevin began his career in the cigar industry as a retail tobacconist, opening Winston’s Humidor in 2009. As a Certified Retail Tobacconist, his passion for cigars grew, as did his desire to become hands on with tobacco both in the fields and factories. Learning firsthand from industry giants throughout Central America and the Caribbean, and developing extensive personal tasting notes, Kevin has dedicated himself to the art of tobacco.﻿

This passion, or some might say obsession, to recapture the shared experience of enjoying a relaxing smoke with his grandfather resulted in the combination of Wade's experience and discriminating palate with the skill, knowledge, and business acumen of Kevin, to create their first offerings. The idea of three distinct blends originated over a three-course dinner wherein a separate wine and cigar were enjoyed with each course. Wade considers the blends almost "as children -- each with a different personality but a source of pride and delight," and is most pleased to introduce the Orinoco 1613 blends. One is sweet, creamy and smooth like the experience of a beignet with coffee; another is nutty and smooth -- perfect for the golf course on a warm afternoon; and the third, has the boldness and flavor to serve as the perfect postlude to a steak or pasta dinner. These are no "gas station" cigars he likes to say, "but premium long filler smokes."﻿
